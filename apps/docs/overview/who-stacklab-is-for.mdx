# Who StackLab Is For

StackLab is designed for developers who want stronger engineering judgment, not just more content.

## Primary Audiences

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Frontend developers" defaultOpen>
    StackLab helps frontend engineers deepen their understanding of runtime behavior, browser concepts, backend interaction, and systems thinking beyond UI work.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Backend developers">
    StackLab helps backend engineers compare architecture patterns, reason about scale and failure, and study practical production tradeoffs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Full-stack developers">
    StackLab helps full-stack builders connect frontend, backend, data, delivery, and product architecture choices in one place.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Interview preparation users">
    StackLab helps users prepare for architecture and system design discussion with behavior-first explanations instead of memorized canned answers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Contributors and educators">
    StackLab gives contributors a structured open-source platform for teaching engineering concepts through docs, simulations, templates, and architecture notes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Secondary Audiences

- indie hackers who need stronger defaults
- technical writers documenting engineering systems
- AI agents working in well-documented engineering repos
