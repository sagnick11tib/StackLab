# AI Contribution Policy

StackLab explicitly allows AI-assisted contribution, but it treats maintainability as the acceptance bar.

<ContributionPrinciples />

## Review Expectations

- the contributor must understand the submitted change
- docs and naming should stay aligned with repo rules
- broad low-context dumps should be broken into smaller coherent edits
- AI should support engineering judgment, not replace it
