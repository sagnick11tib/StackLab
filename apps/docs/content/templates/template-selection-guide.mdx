# Template Selection Guide

Choose templates by product shape first, not by habit.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Landing page">
    Use a focused Next.js landing template when the priority is launch speed,
    strong structure, and a polished public surface.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="SEO-heavy site">
    Use a Next.js SEO-first template when content structure, metadata, and
    long-term discoverability are major constraints.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Dashboard or SaaS">
    Use a dashboard or SaaS template when auth, navigation structure, and
    application shell quality matter more than a marketing-first layout.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Realtime product">
    Use a real-time starter when subscriptions, concurrency, event flows, and
    user-presence behavior are first-class concerns.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Selection Rule

Template pages should help users answer:

- what product am I building
- what defaults do I need today
- what complexity am I willing to accept now
- what will become painful later if I choose the wrong starter
